HireMeFelony

Problem

Currently ex-felons face a barrier due to their past when they try to reintegrate themselves into society.

Content

We want to provide them with important skills related to all aspects of finding employment, be it from applying and interviewing, to how to dress and communicate in a business environment, or even how to manage finances once they have a steady source of income.

Design Process

Together with Coursera, we aim to provide a platform to help these individuals better themselves and become functioning members of society.
