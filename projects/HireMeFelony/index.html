HireMeFelony

Problem

Currently ex-felons face a barrier due to their past when they try to reintegrate themselves into society.

Content

We want to provide them with important skills related to all aspects of finding employment, be it from applying and interviewing, to how to dress and communicate in a business environment, or even how to manage finances once they have a steady source of income.

Design Process

Together with Coursera, we aim to provide a platform to help these individuals better themselves and become functioning members of society.

Weekly Video Lectures

Lessons include weekly lectures that have important information that teach users soft skills that everyone should have in their toolbox.

Accomponying Activities

Complementary to the videos, activities provide another way for users to apply skills that are taught.

Scenarios

Scenarios provide possible situations users may find themselves in that will help them better understand what they may encounter in the future and learn to adapt early and quickly.

Tests To Ensure Comprehension

Tests will be provided at the middle and end of lessons to ensure user comprehensions to give users a bench mark on information retention.
